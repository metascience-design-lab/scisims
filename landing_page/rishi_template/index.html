Crowdsource your research

Rishi will be a web platform that actively encourages scientists to crowdsource their research from ideation to publication. Ultimately, it will resemble a hybrid of Google Business Suite and Wikipedia.

Let a verified crowd of experts peer review your submissions in 14 days. The REVEL open-access archive stores all journal articles, preprints, and even micropublications.
